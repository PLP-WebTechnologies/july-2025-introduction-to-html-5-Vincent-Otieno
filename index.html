Ingredients

  • 1 onion + 1 tomato ‚Äì blended/sliced for sauce
  • 1 cup maize flour + water ‚Äì for ugali
  • 1 lemon ‚Äì for marinating fish
  • 2 tbsp oil ‚Äì for frying & sauce
  • Salt + black pepper

Preparation Steps

  1. Clean the fish, make diagonal cuts, then rub with lemon juice, salt, and black pepper before frying in hot oil until golden brown on both sides (5-7 minutes per side).
  2. Boil salted water in a pot, gradually stir in maize flour while mixing continuously until it forms a thick, firm dough (about 10 minutes).
  3. Once the ugali pulls away from the pot, mold it into a smooth round ball or mound using a wooden spoon.
  4. Serve a portion of ugali alongside the fish and tomato-onion sauce, with extra lemon wedges if desired.
